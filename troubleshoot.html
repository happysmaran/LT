Troubleshoot

The below table contains problems, reasons, and solutions. If your problem is not listed in the table, or the solution provided did’nt work, please contact us at troubleshoot@lightTech.com.

Problem Reason Solution
The device does not start. It could be because the product has no charging, or because the battery’s warranty is gone. Keep the product in charging for at least 20 minutes. If the product still does not start, check for any leakage or smell from the product. If a smell or leakage exists, please contact at +91 2047803457 or email troubleshoot@lightTech.com. Please keep the the product and/or the leakage away from ignition sources. Immediatley unplug the device and evacuate the room the product is in. Wait for Light Technologies personel to arrive and fix the issue.
The device does not turn off It could be because of hardware failure. IE, the product’s hardware can’t communicate with the product’s software, or the software itself can’t turn off the device. For a laptop PC, press Alt, and F4 on the keyboard and a dialog opens. select the desired option, in this case shut down, and press enter. The product turns off. For a VR headset, press the buttons Power, and Menu to turn it off.
An update on the device does not happen or takes a long time. It might take long time because the update was already done previously, the "hold updates" has been turned on in the settings, or There are apps running in the background making updates slow. Turn off all background applications and make sure the update is running the foreground. IE, make the app responsible for updates the only application running on the PC. If updates do’nt happen automatically, go to settings⇒Update And Security⇒Wimplux Update⇒set Hold Updates to off.
The product does not charge It might not charge because the product’s charger is not properly connected, or the charger itself has expired. Please connect the charger properly and make sure that the supply is coming from the socket and is at least 200 Volts. If the product’s charger has expired, please order one online, or buy one from an official Light Technologies store. See the sample label to check where the expiry date is.
